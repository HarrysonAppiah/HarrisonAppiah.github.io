## 🔬 Research Overview

My work focuses on transforming waste streams into valuable materials using thermochemical and green chemistry approaches.

---

## 🧪 Research Areas

### 1. Plastic Recovery from MSW
- Solvent-based extraction of PE and PP  
- Optimization using aromatic solvents (xylene, toluene)

### 2. Biochar & Carbon Materials
- Pyrolysis of biogenic MSW  
- Structural evolution and graphitization  

### 3. Biomass Valorization
- Conversion of xylan to furfural  
- Use of deep eutectic solvents (DES)

---

## ⚙️ Techniques & Instrumentation

- GC-MS  
- DSC / TGA / TMA  
- BET surface area analysis
- FTIR / TMA /UV-VIS

## 🎯 Research Impact

- Advancing circular economy solutions  
- Reducing landfill waste  
- Developing sustainable materials
